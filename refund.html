Refund / Cancellation Policy

Consultation fees once paid are generally non-refundable.

In case of appointment cancellation by Bloom Child Counselling Center due to unavoidable circumstances, the paid amount may be refunded or adjusted against a future appointment.

Cancellation requests must be communicated in advance. Refunds, if applicable, will be processed within a reasonable time.

Bloom Child Counselling Center reserves the final authority regarding refunds and cancellations.
